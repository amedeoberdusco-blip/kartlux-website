About us

A Luxembourg platform for international investment structures.

Kart Lux S.A. operates from Luxembourg, one of Europe’s leading financial centres, providing a corporate platform for investment holding, structured finance and securitisation-related activities.

The company is designed to support transparent, efficient and documented structures for investors, entrepreneurs and institutional counterparties.

Securitisation

Segregated structures for dedicated transactions.

Kart Lux S.A. may establish securitisation structures connected to specific assets, portfolios, receivables or investment opportunities, subject to applicable legal, tax and regulatory requirements.

Dedicated compartments may be used to segregate assets, liabilities and risks related to individual transactions or investment portfolios.

  • Asset and liability segregation
  • Transaction-specific documentation
  • Cross-border investment structuring
  • Clear governance and reporting framework

Governance

Kart Lux S.A. aims to operate with appropriate governance, documentation and due diligence standards, including counterparty identification, transaction review and ongoing administrative oversight.
